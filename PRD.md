# Stance — Skateboarding Progress Tracker  
#App #Skateboarding #Health #Fitness   
  
**Target Audience**: Beginner to advanced skateboarders, self-directed learners, casual and dedicated practitioners, all age groups with safety and progression in mind.  
**Platforms**: iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS.  
**Stack**: Swift, SwiftUI, CoreData  
**Logo: **Cthulhu skating squishily, loading animation kickflip feeble grind  
  
# Core Features  
These features mark the minimum viable product to be released to the App Store initially.  
  
## Notifications  
* Good weather notifications (optimal skate time: 4 p.m., 18°C, low wind)  
* Gear change or maintenance (been skating these wheels for 3 months, flip them)  
  
## Home Tab   
* Skate Readiness Score - Weather and Health Condition  
* Track success rates per stance  
* Recent tricks  
* Current setup tracker  
* Start skate session button  
  
## Health Screen  
* Health stats dashboard  
* Session duration  
* Calories burned  
* Activity trends  
  
## Trick Encyclopedia  
* Comprehensive trick database, basic, flip, grind, and vert categories  
* Offline availability  
* Popularity indicators, flame icon for lots of recent learners  
* Detailed trick pages  
    * Multiple stance support (regular, fakie, nollie, switch)  
    * Progress tracking, haptic feedback  
    * Trick history and invention story  
    * Foot placement diagrams  
    * Video demonstrations, regular and slow motion  
    * Spatial video support  
    * Community tips  
  
## Community  
* Chat  
* Feed/following (user posted tricks?)  
* Competitions  
* News/interviews  
* Spot/Park submissions/tracking  
* Gear reviews, performance, durability, look, feel  
* achievement system?  
  
## Health  
* Nutrition guidance  
* Stretching/warmup routines  
* Fitness programming, strength training, yoga, plyometrics  
* HealthKit integration, workout tracking, activity minutes, rest period monitoring  
* Injury prevention, fall detection, impact tracking  
* Rest recommendations based on HealthKit data.  
  
## Settings  
* Theme customization, dark mode, accent colors  
* feedback, issues, feature requests, missing trick submissions.  
  
## Device Integration  
* Widgets   
    * progress dashboard   
    * current trick status   
    * weather conditions  
* Watch integration  
    * quick progress updates  
    * session controls  
    * haptic metronome
